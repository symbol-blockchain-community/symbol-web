NFTDrive Inc, which uses the Symbol blockchain to provide a range of services including the use of fully-on-chain NFT, has announced that it will be exhibiting at HackOsaka2024 in Osaka on 26 November.
https://x.com/nftdrive_jp/status/1855543130411696536

##  Hack Osaka 2024～ Link Innovation to Our Future ～
* Date and Time : Tuesday, 26 November 2024, 10:30 - 18:30 (scheduled)
* Venue : [Knowledge Capital Congrès Convention Center](https://maps.app.goo.gl/xEtc4Gfuob14KZjk6)
![HackOsaka-1.jpeg](../images/Hack_Osaka_1_c774eb0f34.jpeg)

Hack Osaka 2024: Now in its 12th year, this international innovation conference will focus on 'Linking Innovation to Our Future'. With an expanded venue, the event showcases global innovators, start-ups and university research. It's one of Kansai's largest innovation festivals, bringing together a global community.

The event is about innovation and technology, not just blockchain and crypto assets. However, NFTDrive Inc has come up with an innovation and is doing business with blockchain. As an innovative company, it is not surprising that they are involved in this event. Rather, it could be proof that blockchain-based business is being embraced by the general public: as the event title "Link Innovation to Our Future" suggests, NFTDrive Inc's inventions are creating the future.
