Event title: [NexTech Week Tokyo 2025 6th Blockchain Expo Autumn Edition](https://www.nextech-week.jp/hub/ja-jp/visit/bc.html)
Date: 8th (Wed) to 10th (Fri) October 2025, 10:00 to 17:00
Venue: Makuhari Messe Hall 4-6
Admission: Free (pre-registration required)

![BlockchainExpo 2025Autum.jpeg](../images/Blockchain_Expo_2025_Autum_3f867f9abb.jpeg)

A total of 14 companies and organisations, including NEMTUS, a non-profit organisation that aims to promote and develop NEM and Symbol technologies in Japan, and companies/organisations that are developing businesses using Symbol, will jointly exhibit at the event "NexTech Week Tokyo 2025 6th Blockchain Expo Autumn Edition", the largest event in Japan for the latest blockchain technologies and services. 

They will showcase the products and services of companies/organisations using the Symbol blockchain, comprehensively highlight the technical potential of the Symbol blockchain and the value of its social implementation, and explore the adoption and collaboration.

The AI, Quantum Computing and Human Resourcing in Digital Development Support EXPO will also be held at the same venue. And the showcase is expected to be an excellent opportunity for Symbol to reach out the many business people interested in the latest digital technologies.

## Examples of Symbol usecases are featured in the case study seminar

### Blockchain Case Studies 6
Date: 9th (Thu) October 2025, 12:00 to 12:40
"Preventing the spread of 'secondary information disasters' during crises! Broadcasting × blockchain: tackling fake and misinformation."

This presentation will introduce a method using IP Datacast (IPDC) and blockchain technology to enable independent decision-making and action, without dependence of telecommunications infrastructure. It will also explore the potential social applications of this method. This will be followed by a discussion incorporating insights from security experts.
