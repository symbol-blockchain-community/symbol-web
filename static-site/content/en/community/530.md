NFTDrive Inc., which develops services using the Symbol blockchain, has received the "Technology Award" from the Digital Archive Promotion Consortium for its work in the field of digital archives.

NFTDrive Inc. makes it possible for anyone to verify authenticity by storing the data itself directly on the blockchain.

> [Announcement by NFTDrive Inc.](https://x.com/nftdrive_jp/status/2036760002275201467?s=20)

<img width="594" height="241" alt="Image" src="../images/NFTDrive_Digital_Archive_Award_dd805143.webp" />

With this award, the company's CEO, Mr. Nakashima, stated that "digital archives until now have been based on trust, but in their true form, records only come into existence when they can be verified." NFTDrive aims to shift this paradigm "from trust to verification" and to make responsibility for records visible.

> [Comment from CEO Nakashima (@Bootarou_0x00)](https://x.com/Bootarou_0x00/status/2036762534175445356?s=20)
