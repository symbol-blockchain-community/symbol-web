The archive of Part 3, 'Discussion with external members', from the daytime session of 'XYMPOSIUM 2025', which took place on 19 September 2025, has been released on YouTube.

![Xymposium2025Archive_3.jpeg](../images/Xymposium2025_Archive_3_9fd4cfbc27.jpeg)

NEMTUS hosted a panel discussion, inviting participants from outside the NEM/Symbol community to discuss the following topics: Symbol's current position; the latest developments in ZKPs; the criteria for becoming a 'chosen chain'; and the broader blockchain landscape and future outlooks within each panellist's core area of expertise.

Speakers
・[Yuta Hayakawa](https://x.com/yi_hayakawa?s=20)  / Founder, CEO at WAVEE Inc. 
・[Tourou](https://x.com/TourouJPN?s=20)  / Crypto Hiroba、Founder (@TourouJPN) 
・[Keisuke Funatsu](https://x.com/keisukefunatsu?s=20)  / blockchain developer
