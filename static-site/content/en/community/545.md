Atomos-Seed LLC has announced a new project, "Mini Republic Story."

> ["Mini Republic Story" project launched | A Web3 process economy that turns the "process" of every kind of value into assets](https://prtimes.jp/main/html/rd/p/000000008.000094413.html)

![Image](../images/Mini_Republic_Story_704132ea.webp)

## 1. Project overview
"Mini Republic Story" is an AI x blockchain-powered Web3 comprehensive solution that turns the "process" of all economic activities into assets and enables diverse communities to be operated as pseudo "independent nations."

* **Launch date:** Wednesday, April 1, 2026
* **Concept:** "Accelerating smiles with technology"
* **Background:** It updates the spirit of the 1980s "mini republic boom" with modern technology. Rather than valuing only completed products, it seeks value in the stories of trial and error (the process) and aims to pass them on to the next generation.

## 2. Five core functions
It combines blockchain (Symbol and others) with AI to build highly transparent evaluation and a sustainable economic sphere.

1.  **Valuing the process:** It records even technologies and failures on the blockchain and tokenizes them.
2.  **AI-assisted operations:** AI analyzes supply-demand balance and community enthusiasm to optimize the ecosystem.
3.  **Independent economic sphere:** It circulates through original points, crypto assets (XYM, ETH), and stablecoins (JPYC).
4.  **"Diplomacy" between republics:** Different republics collaborate to create a "relationship economy" that goes beyond the related population.
5.  **Infrastructure contribution rewards:** It provides a mechanism for staking rewards (harvesting) through node operation.

## 3. Five steps from "nation-building" to "return" (introduction, development, twist, conclusion + return)
It defines a unique cycle that drives the process economy.

* **[Introduction] Declaration of nation-building:** Sharing the vision, issuing passport NFTs, and assigning roles.
* **[Development] Nation-building:** Sharing hidden struggles and challenges, analyzing data from experiences and contributions, and turning them into value.
* **[Twist] Operating value:** Turning failure into fuel for success and driving the economy through "diplomacy" with the outside world.
* **[Conclusion] Holding festivals:** Celebrating milestones that fuse digital and real experiences, and sharing results.
* **[Return] Circulation and succession:** Returning the value gained to the next generation, local communities, and educational institutions, then moving into the next loop.

## 4. Deployment roadmap and use cases

### Main lineup
* **First release:** "[Washoku Republic](https://prtimes.jp/main/html/rd/p/000000012.000099123.html)" (founded on March 31, 2026)
* **Founding decided:** Muscle World, Ukiyo-e, Smiles
* **Planned for founding:** Manga / Anime, Film, Music, Cuteness, Symbol (XYM)
* **Under consideration:** Local municipalities, specialties, regional brands, and more

### Recommended utilization strategies
* Brand enhancement and community marketing for companies
* Regional revitalization and the creation of related populations by local governments
* Digital archiving, protection, and operation of traditional techniques and creative content

## 5. Technology and payment infrastructure
* **Blockchain:** Symbol, Ethereum, and others
* **Payment methods:** Japanese yen, proprietary tokens, crypto assets (XYM), stablecoins (JPYC), and more, selectable by each republic.
