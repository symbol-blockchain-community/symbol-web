A stamp rally project using the "Machi Meguri" stamp rally application developed by Opening Line Inc. will be held at the "DAIDOGEI World Cup in Shizuoka". (DAIDOGEI means "Street Performance" in Japanese)
[大道芸ワールドカップのNFTスタンプラリーを実施いたします！](https://daidogei.com/news/index.php?e=101)

![machi-meguriDAIDOGEI.jpeg](../images/machi_meguri_DAIDOGEI_a70604ef40.jpeg)

The World Cup has been held in Shizuoka City, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan, every year in early November since 1992, and is one of the largest street performance festivals in Asia, attracting more than 1.5 million people during the event period.

By participating in the NFT Stamp Rally, where participants enjoy visiting the venue and collecting stamps, they can exchange their stamps for special prizes. (The stamp rally period: From 2 November 2023 to 5 November)
