Proof of Your Life Ltd has been established to continue the development of its DID:decentralised identity platform, 'Proof of Your Life', based on the Symbol blockchain.

![Proof of Your Life_2.webp](../images/Proof_of_Your_Life_2_e480a5e840.webp)

> * [Announcement from Proof of Your Life Ltd](https://x.com/proof_of_your_x/status/1775380783194308948?s=20)
> * [Official website](https://proof-of-your-life.io)
> * [Service page](https://web.dev.proof-of-your-life.io)

It is said that 'Proof of Your Life' is a DID platform that uses the Symbol blockchain and allows users to disclose profile information such as social media accounts, nationality, location, nickname, name, date of birth, education, work experience, qualifications and event attendance information depending on their privacy level. The platform service is currently under development.

![Proof of Your Life.svg](../images/Proof_of_Your_Life_5fdfd170d9.svg)

### Content of its business
Development and operation of the DID platform Proof of Your Life, other systems development, project management and consultancy.
