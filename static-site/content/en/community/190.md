NEMTUS, a non-profit organisation that aims to promote and develop NEM and Symbol technology in Japan, will be exhibiting at the technical book event “Gijutsushoten 15” to be held on November 12, 2023.

**Books for sale**
- Symbol KaitaiShinsho
- Quick learning Symbol blockchain JavaScript edition
- Quick learning Symbol blockchain C# edition
- Monthly NEMTUS: Lightning Talks Collection of materials vol.1
- Monthly NEMTUS: Lightning Talks Collection of materials vol.2

### About the Gijutsushoten15
- Date and time: Sunday, November 12, 2023 11:00-17:00
- Ikebukuro Sunshine City Exhibition Hall D (Bunka Kaikan Building 2F)
- Participation: Free admission  
**Admission ticket required. Admission tickets will not be issued at the venue on the day of the event, so please register as a member from the [official website](https://techbookfest.org/event/tbf15) and prepare your admission ticket.**

### What is Gijutsushoten?
> Gijutsushoten is a festival where you can discover new technologies.
> The Technical Book Encyclopedia was started with the desire to "help spread the use of various technologies". They see the Technical Book Encyclopedia as a place for people involved in technology, where exhibitors can share their know-how with a focus on technical books, and where visitors can find interesting technical books that can only be found here.
