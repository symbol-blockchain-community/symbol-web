To round off 2025, NEMTUS is hosting a special Christmas event🎄: a 'Mokumoku-kai' (Tech Meetup work session) and party!

'Mokumoku-kai' is a Japanese term meaning 'tech meet-up', where participants work independently on coding or study tasks for a set amount of time. The event usually begins with a brief introduction and goal-setting session. You then work in silence before sharing your results.

It's a relaxed yet productive day where engineers, planners and designers interested in hackathons and Web3 development projects can either work quietly on their own projects or engage in technical discussions and networking with NEMTUS members and other participants.✨

A tech meet-up (Moku-Moku Work Session) will take place until midday, and you are welcome to join at any point during this time. After that, there will be a 🎅Christmas party🎂. (Planning to have a bingo tournament.)

Let's take a look at the past year and identify potential activities and collaborations for the year ahead!
