As a new initiative, NEMTUS, an NPO for the promotion of NEM/Symbol blockchain technology, is organising the NEMTUS Community Meet-ups.
NEMTUS members visit various locations in Japan to communicate with local Blockchain/NEM/Symbol community members, deep into the blockchain.
There are no specific qualifications required to participate and participation is encouraged on a casual basis.
