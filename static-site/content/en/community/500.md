## About the Everyday Symbol
Everyday Symbol is a site that compiles venues where accept XYM payment, the cryptocurrency of the Symbol blockchain.

The cryptocurrency used for payment at venues is XYM. However, it is becoming common to refer to it as "Symbol payments".

Although Symbol is not a blockchain designed for settlement purposes, its fast transaction speed, relatively low fees and availability of wallets and QR code generation apps for sending and receiving XYM have contributed to the growing adoption of Symbol payments.

The new Everyday Symbol site categorises venues and also allows users to search for them via map.

## About the Symbol Ecosystem
One of the key features of the Symbol blockchain is the 'Harvesting', which allows users to earn block rewards. The platform also provides instructions on how to perform 'Delegated Harvesting', which allows you to harvest by delegating your stake to a third-party node, without having to set up your own.
