An online forum organised by the [NFT Tourism Association of Japan](https://nfttourism.net) on Thursday 23 and Friday 24 January 2025 will bring together 20 companies that are creating the future of the tourism industry and present their case studies. 
From companies developing services using the Symbol blockchain, [FOOD NFT CONSORTIUM](https://food-nft.io) will present on 'Food NFT and local development'. [Opening Line, Inc](https://www.opening-line.co.jp) will present on 'The benefits and opportunities of turning city attractions into NFTs'.

![Tourism x Web3 Forum 2025 Winter.png](../images/Tourism_x_Web3_Forum_2025_Winter_9bb680ebec.png)

The NFT Tourism Association of Japan aims that the combination of NFT/Web3 and tourism can promote solutions to various industry issues and aims to update the industry (e.g. tourism destination management, building relationships with travellers, improving experience value, preserving digital value, etc.) through NFT/Web3.
And they will contribute to the development of the tourism industry by disseminating knowledge related to NFT, Web3, including DAO or Metaverse, and by creating a platform for matching operators and business creation.

## Event overview

・Organising：NFT Tourism Association of Japan
・Date and time: 23 and 24 Jan 2025, scheduled for around 13:30-17:30 on both days.
・Online：Zoom will be used
