NFTDrive Inc., well known for NFTDrive and NFTDriveEX, and popular illustrator Mina Toujou, who publishes [XYM Comic](https://symbol-community.com/community/44) series, have announced that they will appear at KYOTO INTERNATIONAL MANGA ANIME FAIR 2023(KYOMAF). 
At the main exhibition site, KYOMAF Stage, the South Hall of ROHM Theatre Kyoto, there will be some merchandise from Shion, who is also a publicity ambassador.  

### About the KYOTO INTERNATIONAL MANGA ANIME FAIR 2023
[event outline]
- **Date** 16 (Sat) - 17 (Sun) September 2023.
- **Venue** 
  - 【Main site】[South Hall of ROHM Theatre Kyoto](https://rohmtheatrekyoto.jp/en/) 09:00～17:00 （Until 16:00 on 17） ※Admission until 30 minutes before closing.
  - 【2nd site】[Kyoto International Manga Museum](https://kyotomm.jp/en/) 10:30～19:30 ※Admission until 30 minutes before closing.
  - 【Satellite】[Kyoto Museum of Crafts and Desigh](https://kmtc.jp/en/) 09:00～17:00　（Until 16:00 on 17th） ※Admission until 30 minutes before closing.
  - 【Umekoji park】[Kyoto Soundtrack festival (Kyo-bansai)](https://www.kyobansai.com/en) 16 (Sat) September 2023. Open 13:00 / Start 14:00 / End 18:45
- **Organising** KYOTO INTERNATIONAL MANGA ANIME FAIR Executive Committee, Kyoto City

### About the KYOMAF
KYOMAF is the abbreviation for the Kyoto International Manga Anime Fair, one of the few large-scale manga/anime events in west Japan. 
Publishers, TV stations and video manufacturers will be exhibiting, promoting their latest works, selling goods and holding stage events.
