NEMTUS, a non-profit organisation that aims to promote and develop NEM and Symbol technology, will be exhibiting at the 10th Technical Book Doujinshi Expo to be held on 12 May 2024.
Please visit, try and enjoy NEM/Symbol blockchain.

### Books for sale
* Symbol Kaitai Shinsho
* Quick learning Symbol blockchain JavaScript version / C# version
* Monthly NEMTUS Lightning Talk material collection vol.1
* Monthly NEMTUS Lightning Talk material collection vol.2

### Details of the 10th Technical Book Doujinshi Expo
* Date and time: Sunday, 12 May 2024 11:00 ~ 16:00
* Venue: Ota-ku Industrial Plaza PiO Large Exhibition Hall, 1-20-20 Minami Kamata, Ota-ku, Tokyo [GoogleMAP](https://goo.gl/maps/2J282yVtzG3T5f398)
* Participation: Free admission [Participation application form](https://gishohaku.connpass.com/event/315446/)

### What is the Technical Book Doujinshi Expo?
> Technical Book Doujinshi Expo (Technical Book Expo) is an on-site sales event for technical books (doujinshi). In addition to IT, which covers a wide range of technologies such as science and engineering, mathematics, design and management. This event was born out of a desire to support and strengthen the output of engineers. We aim to be a place where both beginners and veterans can relax, deepen their knowledge, make friends and grow while interacting in a relaxed manner.
Source: [Technical Book Doujinshi Expo] (https://gishohaku.dev/)
