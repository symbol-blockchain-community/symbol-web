Record your footprints, such as favourite cafés, places visited, memories of family trips and so on. Make your life count, have a proof of your life!

Cherish your precious memories with the "[My Ashiato](https://apple.co/42ADWTM)". 
(*"Ashiato" means "footprint" in Japanese.*)

This app allows you to plot your precious moments on a map, add diary-like comments, and securely record your feelings on the blockchain. 
![My Ashiato.png](../images/My_Ashiato_973c95739f.png)

## About the "My Ashiato"
Remember every moment with "My Ashiato". This app allows you to create a personalised map of your life by pinning your favourite places and special memories with text, photos and videos. Capture the emotions, feelings and scenes of each moment and look back on them at any time.
![My Ashiato pic.png](../images/My_Ashiato_pic_9171795ce9.png)


## Main functions
### What can be recorded
- Pins (15 times/day)
- Text (5 texts/day)
- Photographs (5 photographs/day)
- Video (5 videos/day)

Location and time are automatically associated with recordings. Recorded data can be stored in encrypted form on the Symbol blockchain from the following day.

The data recorded on the Symbol blockchain is text data, recording time and recording location. Photos and videos are stored locally as application data within the app.

### Data management
- Daily data limit: 992 bytes (compressed)
- When recording data to Symbol, it must be done manually within 7 days from the following day. 
- After 7 days, unprocessed data is automatically deleted.

### Account information
- Check Symbol account information, language settings (15 languages available) and monitor usage.
- Existing accounts cannot be imported, please create a new account in "My Ashiato" initial settings.
- When you set up your account, a view code is generated that is used to decrypt the data you have encrypted and stored in Symbol. If the view code is known to others, they may be able to see the recorded data, so please keep it safe with the same care as a private key or mnemonic.

### Limitations
- Recording to the same location is limited to once per day.
- GPS accuracy may cause misalignment.
- Transaction fees of XYM, the native currency of the Symbol blockchain, are required for recording on the Symbol blockchain.
