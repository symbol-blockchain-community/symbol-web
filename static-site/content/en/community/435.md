[Opening Line, Inc](https://www.opening-line.co.jp), a forerunner in creative blockchain solutions, has announced the upcoming release of its book 'Practical Applications Built with the Symbol Blockchain'.

![OpeningLine_book.png](../images/Opening_Line_book_50cab245ec.png)

This is a practical guide that details application development using TypeScript and Python, taking advantage of the technical benefits of the Symbol blockchain.
It's useful for both Symbol developers and those with experience of other blockchains or an interest in Web3 services. The book covers various spaces such as examples and design context for DAO demo applications, as well as cross-chain integration.

### Release Schedule
- Bookstores: 9 May 2025
- Amazon：13 May 2025
