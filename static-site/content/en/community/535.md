## Toward a future of tamper-resistant records: BEYOND, a social infrastructure where anyone can verify the reliability of records

<img width="1280" height="720" alt="Image" src="../images/BEYOND_by_NFTDrive_ab882910.webp" />

NFTDrive Inc. is working on technologies that use blockchain to store and utilize data in ways that make tampering difficult. NFTDrive has announced a new project called "BEYOND", and crowdfunding is currently underway.

> Please visit the crowdfunding page here
> [Toward a future of tamper-resistant records: BEYOND, a social infrastructure where anyone can verify the reliability of records](https://camp-fire.jp/projects/938528/view)

1. Project overview and objective
 "BEYOND" is a next-generation social infrastructure that uses blockchain technology to manage and verify all kinds of data, such as education, healthcare, and social media, as "tamper-proof proof."
 It aims to update today's world, where people have no choice but to trust organizations or individuals, into a society where anyone can directly verify authenticity through data.

2. Challenges it addresses and strengths

   Challenge: The limitations of existing infrastructure, including the effort required to issue certificates, concerns over data reliability, and dependence on the managing entity.

   Strength: Ownership of patented "full on-chain technology." Strong technological capability that has been highly regarded in the digital archive field, including winning the JBA Award.

3. Future outlook and use of support
   Activity: UI/UX design and browser development are underway for a prototype release in June 2026.

   Use of funds: Development costs and advertising and promotional expenses.

   Return: Providing an "experience of participating in future infrastructure," such as inscribing the record of support itself on the blockchain.


## [Public demonstration of BEYOND by NFTDrive](https://note.com/tender_echium972/n/n3c46cd00e185)

<img width="1024" height="682" alt="Image" src="../images/BEYOND_by_NFTDrive_demo_df0cffe6.webp" />

Three demonstration scenarios
 
1. A network for storing clinical trial data in a verifiable state
From participant registration to medication administration and test results, every step can leave an on-chain audit trail.

1. Automobile maintenance certificate network
Maintenance history becomes a tamper-resistant record and serves as an interface for responsibility and proof.

1. Graduation certificate
It is not established by a document that certifies graduation, but by holding credit NFTs and fulfilling the prescribed completion requirements, it is established as an on-chain state. In addition, this certificate is established based on token ownership status without relying on personal information.
Rather than being "a mechanism for issuing certificates," it functions as a certificate through "a mechanism for proving state."

## Summary of BEYOND

It is a multi-network compatible verification infrastructure developed by NFTDrive Inc., a structure for "seeing, aggregating, and verifying" blockchains.

Without depending on a single chain, it traverses multiple Catapult-based networks and makes the existence of data, its integrity, and the location of responsibility visible.

Designed with a Write-Verify-Audit philosophy, it focuses on the reliability of records themselves.

Rather than hiding blockchain in the background, it presents it up front as a usable UI and creates infrastructure that puts anyone in a state where verification is possible.
