Hiroyuki Goto (President of Atomos-Seed LLC, President of the NPO NEMTUS, Co-Chairman of the Food NFT Consortium) will appear at 'Explanation of the Evolution and Latest Trends of the Web in the Web3 Era', presented by FIVESTARinteractive Inc. 

In the latest move by the Japanese government, following the "Basic Policy for Economic and Financial Management and Reform 2022", the Cabinet approved the "Basic Policy for Economic and Financial Management and Reform 2023 (Basic Policy 2023)" on 16 June 2023. 

> To develop the environment for the use of Web 3.0 tokens and the revitalisation of the content industry, taking due account of user protection, and to take the necessary measures to broaden the base of actors and ideas for the realisation of a decentralised digital society. 
[「経済財政運営と改革の基本方針2023」（骨太方針2023）](https://www5.cao.go.jp/keizai-shimon/kaigi/cabinet/honebuto/2023/decision0616.html)

As part of the national commitment, the three lectures will provide those involved in web production with a detailed explanation of the cutting-edge web3, which continues to change and evolve at an accelerating pace behind the scenes of the web. 

It is hoped that these lectures will enable attendees to learn how to integrate this new wave of blockchain technology into their own products, in new and important ways, and that they will bring back a lot of feedback. 

The speaker, Hiroyuki Goto, has gained a high level of insight through his career from the early era of blockchain technology and experience working for blockchain companies. He is currently the president of Atomos-Seed LLC, a content platform DX/TX company, and the president of NEMTUS, the only NPO in the Japanese blockchain industry, as well as the co-chairman of the Food NFT Consortium.
