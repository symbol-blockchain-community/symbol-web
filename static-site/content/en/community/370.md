[Opening Line, Inc.](https://www.opening-line.co.jp)in collaboration with the [Blockchain Collaborative Consortium](https://bccc.global) (hereinafter referred to as BCCC), has announced that it will use the blockchain-based stamp collection rally service "Peace Collection" at the Blockchain EXPO to be held at Makuhari Messe from 20 to 22 November 2024 (Wednesday to Friday).

## About the "Peace Collection"
This is a new type of stamp collecting rally where participants collect different experiences as 'pieces' (stamps) to complete a collection. In this system, pieces are shared across multiple collections, connecting them across regions, companies, past and future, real and virtual.
Proof of collection is issued as a "SoulBound token" (SBT). This makes it easy for anyone, anywhere to prove ownership and link to different systems.　
![peace collection.jpeg](../images/peace_collection_91020adcbc.jpeg)
>  https://lp.p-coll.com

### Features
* No personal information required
* Record experiences in different ways (GPS, QR, link click)
* Inter-regional connections can be created by linking collections (courses)
* Can confirm ownership of certificates outside of the piece collection

## Details of the stamp collection rally project
The collection will visit seven stands of BCCC member companies, and after completing a simple mission at each company stand, the QR code can be scanned to receive a 'piece'. Those who collect all the 'pieces' will receive a prize at the BCCC stand.

## Date, time and location of the event
Dates: 20 (Wed) - 22 (Fri) November 2024
Time: 10:00-17:00
Venue: BCCC member company booths at Makuhari Messe
