There is an active community, particularly in Japan which using the Symbol blockchain for development by many individual developers, various companies and organisations to create and deliver products, services or social goods in our daily lives. This article presents some examples of use by companies and organisations.
（*At the time of writing, August 2024. Names of companies and organisations are listed in alphabetical order in Japanese.*）

## 2. [NFTDrive Inc.](https://nftdrive.net)
![logo5.png](../images/logo5_87154597be.png)

### Products & Services

* [**NFTDrive**](https://nftdrive.net/?nav=nftdrive)
  * An automated tool for uploading digital data of various types (images, videos, documents, PDFs, HTML, etc.) to the blockchain and issuing fully on-chain NFTs. Fully-on-chain NFTs are highly tamper-resistant and persistent NFTs that store all data related to the NFT on the blockchain, with tokens and digital data tied together, rather than storing digital data off the blockchain using traditional IPFS or other methods. To improve data tamper resistance, NFTDrive has developed a completely unalterable data area by using the world's first private key abrogation method.（*PATPEND）
* [**NFTDriveEX**](https://nftdrive.net/?nav=ex)
  * An NFT-specific wallet made in Japan, developed with the aim of enabling P2P transfer of fully-on-chain NFT for all users.
* [**HAYABUSA**](https://nftdrive.net/?nav=hayabusa)
  * A fully on-chain generative NFT creation tool. The development of a single NFG file method that combines random map data and image data into a single NFG file allows thousands of pieces of generative data to be completed in just one write to the Symbol blockchain, while saving on gas fees. FreeMint or SaleMint are also possible, and MintSite comes with Allow List support and Reveal functionality, and can be used for most chains that support EVM. If users do not need to create a fully-on-chain NFT, they can also create off-chain generative NFTs from within GoogleDrive.
* [**Space**](https://nftdrive.net/?nav=space)
  * The Space is the place where the NFTs issued by the NFTDrive can be shared. Users can view and bid on the different types of assets on offer. With NFTDriveEX, users can start sharing immediately without the need for complicated logins etc. Users simply register their address and there is no need to deposit a certain amount and no application process to transfer NFTs.

## Extra. Symbol Payments
![XYM City image.jpeg](https://cms.symbol-community.com/uploads/XYM_City_image_c8f51ccaef.jpeg)

* [**Everyday Symbol**](https://everyday-symbol.studio.site) (Japanese)
* [**Symbol Payment Map**](https://lab-radiointerference-symbol.my.canva.site/symbol-payment-map) (English)
This website is a list of venues that accept payments in XYM, the native currency of the Symbol blockchain.
The crypto asset used for payments is XYM. However, it is increasingly being referred to as 'Symbol Payments'.
Symbol is not a blockchain designed specifically for payments, but the speed of transfer, relatively low transaction fee and variety of wallets used to send and receive XYM, as well as QR code-generating applications for invoices, have helped Symbol payments become popular. In addition, the development of the tap to pay device, "CryptoPiTouch", by community volunteers is currently underway, and a "CryptoPiTouch" prototype device was shown at the Blockchain Expo Spring in May 2024 to demonstrate how it works, with the application and hardware currently being created for completion. Once completed, the system will be piloted at Symbol Payments venues.
