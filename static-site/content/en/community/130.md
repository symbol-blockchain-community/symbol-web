The 15th Ingredient  Meetup TOKYO is a gathering of manufacturers, artisans and creators involved in manufacturing to explore the future of the dining table under the theme "The Future of the Dining Table - What We Eat" will be held.


At the venue, attendees can try and taste the food NFT "Almost sea urchin" developed by Ajikaori Strategic Research Institute and Kagoshima Highball.
Overview of Material Meetup TOKYO vol.15


### Overview of Material Meetup TOKYO vol.15
- **Date:** 08/25/2023 (Friday)
- **Time:** 19:00–21:00 (doors open at 18:30)
- **Location:** FabCafe MTRL 2F, Dogenzaka Pier Building 2F, 1-22-7 Dogenzaka, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo [MAP](https://goo.gl/maps/dTyJXoyP2sv)
- **Capacity:** 60 people
- **Participation fee:** 1,000 yen (includes 1 drink and tasting) * Receipt is available
