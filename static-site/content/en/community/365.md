A joint research presentation by Hiroyuki Goto, representative of Atomos-Seed LLC, and Michio Nakashima, representative director of NFTDrive Inc. will be given at the 9th Research Conference of the Japan Society for Digital Archive.

![Digital Archive.jpg](../images/Digital_Archive_097e5062ee.jpg)

* Date Saturday, 2 November 2024
* Time 14:35-14:50
* Venue 201, School of Law Bldg., Hongo Campus, University of Tokyo
* Content 
A Practical Research on Blockchain-Based Digital
Archives Utilizing Public Domains and Food:
A System Evolving from Cost Reduction and Visualization of Value
and Valuing Data to Value Expansion
