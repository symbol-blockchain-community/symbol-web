NEMTUS, a non-profit organization NEM Technology Promotion Association, will be on stage at Open Seminar 2023@Kagawa on Saturday, September 9, 2023. Presentations will be given by speakers such as Python, PostgresSQL, and security, and NEMTUS will make a presentation on blockchain. We are calling for people living in the neighborhood to participate because it is an offline-only seminar.

### Seminar Details
- Date and time: September 9, 2023 (Sat) 13:00-17:00 (NEMTUS will be on stage from 13:55-14:30)
- Location: Takamatsu Symbol Tower in front of JR Takamatsu Station (2-1 Sunport, Takamatsu City, Kagawa Prefecture) e-Topia Kagawa (4F entrance) BB Square (5F) [GoogleMAP](https://goo.gl/maps/fUVnxLcNmfT1qYZe6)
- Organizer: Setouchi Linux User Group (STLUG)
- [Participation Application](https://osk.connpass.com/event/292551/)

### What is Open Seminar?
It is a non-profit free seminar for IT engineers held in the Chugoku and Shikoku regions since 2003.
It is currently held in Kagawa, Okayama, Hiroshima and Ehime prefectures. 
It is planned and run by volunteers from local technical user communities.
