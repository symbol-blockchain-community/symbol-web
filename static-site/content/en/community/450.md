## Event overview
On 19 July, [NEMTUS](https://nemtus.com) will host a hands-on workshop in Ginowan City Okinawa to learn how to develop blockchain-based applications.

![Meetup "Play with Blockchain" @CODE BASE Okinawa.png](../images/Meetup_Play_with_Blockchain_CODE_BASE_Okinawa_64d622ee29.png)

### Meetup "Play with Blockchain" @CODE BASE Okinawa
* Date and time :  19th July 2025 (Sat) 13:00 - 17:00
* Venue : [CODE BASE Okinawa](https://www.protosolution.co.jp/codebase/)
* Address : [Proto Ginowan Okinawa Headquarters No. 2 Building 2F, 7-10-14 Oyama, Ginowan city, Okinawa](https://www.google.com/maps/@26.284167,127.744694,998m/data=!3m1!1e3?hl=ja&entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI1MDYwNC4wIKXMDSoASAFQAw%3D%3D)

* What to bring: Laptop (Mac or Windows), power cord
