XYMPOSIUM 2024 will take place on Friday 27 September at the [Tokyo Node](https://www.tokyonode.jp).

> [Announcement by Symbol X account](https://x.com/thesymbolchain/status/1806128907680399867)
> [Announcement by NEM X account](https://x.com/nemofficial/status/1806128784304959612)

![Xymposium2024_Tokyo_NODE.jpeg](../images/Xymposium2024_Tokyo_NODE_215ee30cb3.jpeg)

XYMPOSIUM is a coined word, a combination of XYM and Symposium, referring to a symposium on the Symbol/NEM.

The previous event was held on Tuesday 19 December 2023 at Tokyo Edition Toranomon and Shinjuku GOX.
At Tokyo Edition Toranomon, information about Symbol/NEM was presented to blockchain business professionals and media, as well as use cases from companies developing their businesses, presentations on the Symbol mobile node, etc.
At the Shinjuku GOX was also held for the community, with the core team visiting Japan to talk about the content of Tokyo Edition Toranomon, and there were also booths set up by Japanese companies using Symbol for users to interact with each other.
