Opening Line Inc. is holding a nationwide Tomatina 2023 event with its self-developed digital stamp collecting app "Machi Meguri". 
By visiting tomato spots (roadside stations) across Japan using the Machi Meguri app, users can get tomato NFTs that can be used for tipping on on the Tomatina day. 
There are 10 types of Tomato NFTs available to get in each region of Japan. To get Tomato NFTs, you will need to have a Symbol address to receive them, so please make sure you have one before you receive them. 
Let's tip Tomato NFTs with friends across the world on the day of Tomatina 2023. 

[Period] 
- August 16th to August 30th, 2023
- Tomato NFT distribution by August 30, 2023
