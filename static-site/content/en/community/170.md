"Hiroyuki Goto's Blockchain Application Production Course" will be held by FIVESTARinteractive Inc. at Rokunana Workshop Ginza in GINZA SCRATCH. Mr. Hiroyuki Goto (President of Atomos-Seed LLC, President of the NPO NEMTUS, Co-Chairman of the Food NFT Consortium) will be speaking as a lecturer.
In this course, you will be able to experience creating a very simple web application and connecting it to the Symbol network.

### What is Rokunana Workshop Ginza?
> Established in 2003, Rokunana Workshop Ginza is a "web production school" run by the web production company Rokunana.

### Course details
* Date and time: Saturday, December 2, 2023, 11:00-18:00
* Speaker: Hiroyuki Goto
* Venue: GINZA SCRATCH Address: Daito Ginza Building 2F, 6-12-13 Ginza, Chuo-ku, Tokyo
* Tuition fee: 29,800 yen (tax included)
* [Application for participation](https://67.org/ws/workshop/detail/0176blockchain.html)
