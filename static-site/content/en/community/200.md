Opening Line, Inc will be exhibiting at the Industrial Exchange Exhibition 2023 being held at Tokyo Big Sight. You can experience the Opening Line, Inc's secure file sharing service "JUGGLE" and stamp collecting rally App "Machimeguri". We also hold events where you can get a book "Symbol Blockchain Application".
At the exhibition booth, you can see the safe and simple file sharing service "JUGGLE" in action and receive an explanation.
