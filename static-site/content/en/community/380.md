[MAHO](https://x.com/mahorobart), a painter, musician and composer of orchestral music known for her work as NFTs in COMSA, will have a solo exhibition in Kanazawa Japan.

The exhibition will also have a music experience inspired by the paintings. This will use MahoroVision, an app that is based on Symbol blockchain. Visitors will be able to appreciate the paintings and listen to them. Visitors will receive a special NFT as well.

- Date: 28 Jan (Tue) - 2 Feb (Sun) 10:00-18:00
- Venue: [Shiiinoki Geihinkan](http://www.shiinoki-geihinkan.jp) Gallery B ([2-1-1 Hirosaka, Kanazawa, Ishikawa](https://maps.app.goo.gl/UfuSB4UHEE7sBb1r7))
- Admission: free

![Memento.jpeg](../images/Memento_d6b08ba8e5.jpeg)

## Exhibition overview
This exhibition will feature artworks from the main collection, which is called the Memento Series. There will be pieces that have been on show before, as well as that have never been published before.

## About the MahoroVision app
When approaching a work of art with the MahoroVision app activated, the system recognises it and displays a description of the work, the artist's thoughts and the content of the music.
Visitors can also receive special NFT by scanning the QR with an account which is created automatically in the MahoroVision app, and can see the NFT in the app as well. However, the application will detect if you have already received an NFT with the same address and you will not be able to receive it more than once.
Please try this at the venue.
