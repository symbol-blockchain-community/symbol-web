On 29 January 2025, the Industry and Data/Content Sub-Committee of the Japan Society for Digital Archive will host a "DA Short Talk".

![DA Short Talk.jpeg](../images/DA_Short_Talk_9cfee76bab.jpeg)

Two companies that are implementing Symbol blockchain in society, Hiroyuki Goto, representative of Atomos-Seed LLC, and Michio Nakashima, representative director of NFTDrive Inc., will give a presentation at the "DA short talk" organised by the Industry and Data/Content Sub-Committee of the Japan Society for Digital Archive.

* Date and Time: 29th January 2025 (Wed) 13:00～15:00
* Appears at : around 13:36~
* Theme: Practical research on blockchain-based digital archiving and it's DAO
* Format: ZOOM webinar
* Target audience: members of the Japan Society for Digital Archive, DAPCON members, DARA participating organisations and other relevant stakeholders.
* Participation: free of charge
