## Summary
### NEM and Symbol-DeFi integration

#### A Bridge to Ethereum
- stXEM and stXYM
- stXEM and stXYM allow NEM and Symbol to bridge to Ethereum while continuing to earn native harvesting rewards.
(Please note that `stXEM` and `stXYM` are currently just internal reference names.)

#### Uniswap support
- Peregrine Partners will operate a Uniswap pool for XYM and XEM.
- The new wallets will enable users to trade without having to interact with smart contracts or complex DeFi interfaces.

#### Native Wallet Integration（Scheduled for release within 2025）
- Both Symbol and NEM wallets with iOS and Android devices.
- Token swaps and Uniswap trading are supported.
- Accelerated addition of functionality on a multi-protocol platform.
