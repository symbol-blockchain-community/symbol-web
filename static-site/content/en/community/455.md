Xymposium 2025  will take place on, 19 September at [TOKYO CULTURE CULTURE](https://tokyocultureculture.com) and [Crypto Lounge GOX](https://cryptoloungegox.com).

> Xymposium is a coined word, a combination of XYM and Symposium, referring to a symposium on the Symbol/NEM.

## Venue

**Daytime session：TOKYO CULTURE CULTURE**
Time: 11:00am to 4:00pm
Theme: Technology, Case Studies, and Discussions
*  Latest announcement from The Symbol Syndicate
*  Case studies of companies that use Symbol
*  Panel discussion with external guests
*  Community Discussion

**Evening session：Crypto Lounge GOX**
Time: 6:30pm to 9:00pm
Theme: Interaction, Presentation, Networking
*  About NEMTUS activities
*  Community member presentation
*  Next NEMTUS Hackathon Announcement
*  Free conversation & merchandise sales

 #### [Announcement by NEMTUS's X account](https://x.com/NemtusOfficial/status/1953764334691389549)
 
 
![Xymposium2025.jpeg](../images/Xymposium2025_bf4f30374e.jpeg)

The previous Xymposium was held on Friday, 27 September 2024, and mainly featured presentations on `KASANE`.
`KASANE` is the codename for Symbol 2.0, a project that expands Symbol and broadens its design aim to support cross-chain compatibility and more complex products. The project evolves the platform's functionality UX to make it more scalable and flexible.
> Click here for more information about Xymposium2024 report. 
→ [Xymposium2024リポート](https://hackmd.io/@Radio-Radio/S1BcJ_T0A)

### Planned content for Xymposium2025

There will be case studies of companies that use Symbol, as well as the latest announcements, proposals for future functions and developments, discussions with external guests and the community discussions.

## Participants must register in advance

Applications for Xymposium 2025 can be submitted in advance via the event's website below.
The event's website → [XYMPOSIUM 2025](https://xymposium.io/2025/)

* Advance registration is required for both the daytime and evening sessions.
* You can participate in either the daytime or evening sessions, or both.
* Participation is free of charge.

#### Recommended for those who
* For those who want to learn about the latest examples and development trends of Symbol blockchain.
* For those seeking opportunities to interact with companies, developers and investors related to Symbol.
* For anyone interested in blockchain and blockchain technology.
* For those interested in community activities and open-source projects.
