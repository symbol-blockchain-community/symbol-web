NEMTUS, a non-profit organisation that aims to promote and develop NEM and Symbol technology in Japan, will be exhibiting at the 9th Technical Book Doujinshi Expo to be held on 25 Nov, 2023.
If you are interested in new technologies and initiatives such as blockchain, NEM/Symbol, etc., please visit and hope you enjoy it.

**[Books scheduled for sale 📕]**
* Symbol Kaitai Shinsho
* Quick learning Symbol blockchain JavaScript version / C# version that can be used in the field from today
* Monthly NEMTUS LT material collection vol.1
* Monthly NEMTUS LT material collection vol.2

### 9th Technical Book Doujinshi Expo Details
* Date and time: Saturday, November 25, 2023 11:00 ~ 16:00
* Venue: Ota-ku Industrial Plaza PiO Large Exhibition Hall Address: 1-20-20 Minami Kamata, Ota-ku, Tokyo [GoogleMAP](https://goo.gl/maps/2J282yVtzG3T5f398)
* Participation: Free admission for general participants
[Participation application connpass](https://gishohaku.connpass.com/event/298538/)

### What is the Technical Book Doujinshi Expo?
> Technical Book Doujinshi Expo (Technical Book Expo) is an on-site sales event for technical books(doujinshi). In addition to IT, which covers a wide range of technologies such as science and engineering, mathematics, design, and management. This event was born out of the desire to support and empower the output of  engineers. We aim to be a place where both beginners and veterans can relax, deepen their knowledge, make friends, and grow while interacting in a relaxed manner.
Source: [Technical Book Doujinshi Expo] (https://gishohaku.dev/)
