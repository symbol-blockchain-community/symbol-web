### 2. [Food NFT Consortium](https://food-nft.io) announcement
#### 「FOOD」×「NFT」：Creation of the Food NFT Market
The Food NFT Consortium has been established to create 'taste data' and 'recipe data' using [Taste & Aroma Strategic Research Institute Co., Ltd.](https://en.mikaku.jp/?_gl=1%2Axgs8tm%2A_ga%2AMTA1NTc2NTE4MS4xNzYyMDg4Njkz%2A_ga_V9RE1H53BY%2AczE3NjIwODg2OTMkbzEkZzAkdDE3NjIwODg2OTMkajYwJGwwJGgw)'s analysis technology. This data will be converted into NFTs to establish a 'Food NFT' market. This will simplify the management of 'digital taste assets' and promote the development and circulation of Food NFTs.

#### Fully on-chain stored FOOD NFT
By storing taste and recipe data in a fully on-chain format and converting it into NFTs on the public Symbol blockchain, the data is permanently recorded and stored, proving its authenticity. This makes it possible to utilise the data efficiently and effectively, as well as the NFTs.
[NFTDrive](https://nftdrive.net) is used to store all taste and recipe data on Symbol and to issue NFTs.

#### About the ONEPLATE App
[ONEPLATE]([https:/](https://oneplate.food-nft.io)/) is a web app where you can distribute, receive and exchange food NFTs. Creating an account and holding NFTs is easy. You can also import an existing Symbol account and use it as a wallet.

### 3. [KTV Software Inc.](https://www.ktvs.co.jp/index.html)  announcement
#### The expanding possibilities of broadcasting and blockchain technology.
* About a system for verifying the reliability of disaster sites that uses IPDC (IP DataCast) and blockchain technology.
IPDC is a technology that uses terrestrial digital broadcasting infrastructure to transmit digital data of various formats. The system ensures stable, wide-area distribution of critical data by transmitting information without requiring an internet connection, even when communication infrastructure is unstable due to disasters. Furthermore, storing this data on a highly tamper-resistant blockchain guarantees its integrity, making the information completely reliable and fully verifiable.
