NEMTUS announces that it will be at Open Source Conference 2025 Kyoto

![OSC.png](../images/OSC_ed9b3af9f8.png)

The Open Source Conference is an exhibition providing the latest information on open source.

## Open Source Conference 2025 Kyoto
Introduction of NEM/Symbol blockchain technology (informations/integrations/study sessions/hackathons/other technical events), distribution of goods, exhibition of technical books.
The conference will be held at
- Date: 3 Aug, 10:00-18:00 (exhibition closes at 16:00)
- Venue: [Kyoto Research Park ](https://www.krp.co.jp/english/) Western Area Bldg#4 B1・2F
- Fee: Free
- Application: [Open Source Conference 2025 Kyoto](https://event.ospn.jp/osc2025-kyoto/)

### NEMTUS
[NEMTUS（NEM+）(特定非営利活動法人NEM技術普及推進会NEMTUS)](https://nemtus.com/) is a non-profit organisation that aims to promote and develop NEM and Symbol technology in Japan.
