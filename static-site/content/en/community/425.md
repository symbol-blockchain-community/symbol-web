## Event overview
On 24 May, [NEMTUS](https://nemtus.com) will host a hands-on workshop in Fukuoka City to learn how to develop blockchain-based applications.
Based on the book "[Quick Learning Symbol JavaScript version](https://nextpublishing.jp/book/17872.html)", a working engineer will provide explanations as the course progresses.
This is an opportunity to learn about, develop and interact with blockchain, let's take a look and get involved.

![blockchainnihureyoukai.JPG](../images/blockchainnihureyoukai_339c600d4a.JPG)

### Meetup "Play with Blockchain" in Fukuoka@Engineer Cafe
* Date and time : Saturday, 24 May 2025, 13:00-17:00
* Venue : [Engineer Cafe - Hacker Space Fukuoka -](https://engineercafe.jp/en)
* Address : [1-15-30, Tenjin, Chuo-ku, Fukuoka City, Japan ](https://maps.app.goo.gl/8maozngweXY1U2Q16)
* Participation fees : Free of charge
