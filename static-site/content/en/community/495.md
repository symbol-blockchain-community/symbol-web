At the 10th Annual Conference of the Japan Society for Digital Archive, Hiroyuki Goto, Representative Director of Atomos-Seed LLC — a company advancing the social implementation of the Symbol blockchain — is to speak. He will discuss the technical, practical and philosophical challenges associated with digital archives, offering insights into potential future frameworks.

![the_Japan_Society_for_Digital_Archive.jpeg](../images/the_Japan_Society_for_Digital_Archive_4979b11257.jpeg)

### Event overview

* Date and Time: 10 January 2026, 10:00–11:30
* Venue: [Hitotsubashi Hall](https://www.hit-u.ac.jp/hall/), Conference Room C
* Theme: A Multi-Layered Approach Towards Enhancing the Value of Digital Archives
* Operations Director
Tetsuro Kamura
Associate Professor, Art Information Center, Tokyo University of the Arts / Director, NPO Linked Open Data Initiative

Hiroyuki Goto will not only speak on 10 January, but will also be a member who manage the Industry and Data/Content Committee's session, 'The Current State of Curation in Digital Archives', at the same research conference on 9 January.
