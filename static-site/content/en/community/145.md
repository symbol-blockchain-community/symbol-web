Draft `16 Sept 2023.` 
FOOD NFT Consortium will hold “FOOD NFT Meetup” on Friday, September 29, 2023 in Nihonbashi, Tokyo. This meetup is for members only and will also be livestreamed online. The event is designed to share information on the latest activities of the Food NFT Consortium and to deepen interaction with members. It will be based on the questions, opinions, ideas, etc. of the members and we will promote the dialogue on Food NFT.　
And also the 'Food NFT Consortium Individual Consultation Session' will be held on the same day (must be booked in advance) and will provide food NFT consultations by topic. This session is also available online (ZOOM).
There will also be on-site support for setting up the ONEPLATE and Discord, and if you bring your own device, you can get help on screen. If you have any problems other than setting up, please feel free to contact us.

- **Date** Friday, September 29, 2023 16:00-17:30(JST)
- **Venue** Nihonbashi, Chuo-ku, Tokyo
Details will be provided in the application guide.
