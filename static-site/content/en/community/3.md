## Games powered by the Symbol blockchain

On this page, we will introduce games that use the Symbol blockchain.  Each games that will be introduced are unique, but what they have in common is that they use the Symbol blockchain. To play, connecting your Symbol account with [SSS Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sss-extension/llildiojemakefgnhhkmiiffonembcan?hl=ja), and also games that can play without Symbol wallet. Please find your favorite game and enjoy the games.  

## [1. The Tower](https://symvolution.com/thetower/)
Developer: Toshi[@toshiya_ma](https://twitter.com/toshiya_ma?s=20&t=umEYHUwDfr7w8SuEGLIQKg)

![symbol_games_introduction_the_tower1.jpg](../images/symbol_games_introduction_the_tower1_3a019f13f6.jpg)
![symbol_games_introduction_the_tower2.jpg](../images/symbol_games_introduction_the_tower2_b6782e560f.jpg)
![symbol_games_introduction_the_tower3.jpg](../images/symbol_games_introduction_the_tower3_230397f10b.jpg)

Character development type 2D top-down action game! There are three types of parameters: life, power, and intelligence. The battle style changes depending on the training policy! Collect coins in randomly generated dungeons, develop characters, and challenge the infinite tower record while competing with other players. The higher the floor, the stronger the enemies, so aim for the top while training them. When you return to the city, not only training but also various NPC players will welcome you. An exchange where commissioned harvesters can exchange gambling deals and real-world Symbol mosaics. It is a game aimed at crushing Symboler's free time with full power, such as a 3D museum compatible with NFT-Drive and COMSA.

This is a mainnet exclusive game. For details on how to operate and the game, please refer to [Instruction](https://kazumasamatsumoto.github.io/the-tower/)  provided by Mr.Matsumoto[OpeningLine](https://www.opening-line.co.jp)  


The knight on the left immediately after starting the game is in charge of explanations, so be sure to talk to him once. The operation method can be found by pressing the [ESC] key during the game.

1XYM is required for character growth and progress from the middle of the hierarchy. The secret key will be encrypted with a password and saved in the game, so please understand this in advance and play with a small amount of XYM (about 10 XYM or less) just in case.

A game Symbol account is created at the start of the game. Please be careful with the private key and save it in your own wallet etc.

### Comments from the developers
It doesn't use any database (other than storing the encryption private key on the terminal), everything is built on Symbol transactions, and all data is pulled from Symbol. The game itself is a typical roguelike game, but I created it with the intention of treating the blockchain like a database. We explored the direction of a unique BC game and created it, so we hope that you can play while imagining how Symbol blockchain is used in addition to the game.
