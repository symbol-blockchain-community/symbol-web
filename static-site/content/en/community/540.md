Food NFT Consortium announced that it will begin offering "Washoku Republic," a comprehensive Web3 process-economy solution, from Tuesday, March 31, 2026.

This service aims to pass on washoku to the next generation and create a new economic sphere by "storing, protecting, and turning into value" the activities, techniques, and knowledge of producers, chefs, consumers, regions, and others involved in washoku.

> [Declaration of Nation-Building: The "Washoku Republic" project begins | A Web3 process economy that adds value to the "process" of washoku](https://prtimes.jp/main/html/rd/p/000000012.000099123.html)

![Image](../images/Washoku_Republic_0c8a4083.webp)

## Exhibiting at "NexTech Week 2026 7th Blockchain EXPO Spring"

Also, at the same time, Food NFT Consortium announced that it will exhibit at "NexTech Week 2026 7th Blockchain EXPO Spring," held from Wednesday, April 15 to Friday, April 17, 2026, and that it will speak at a seminar on Friday the 17th, the final day. Seminar attendees will reportedly receive an advance copy of the "Washoku Republic" white paper as a special benefit.
