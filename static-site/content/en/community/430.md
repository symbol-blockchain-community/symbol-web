## Event overview
On 11 May, [NEMTUS](https://nemtus.com) will host a hands-on workshop in Naha City to learn how to develop blockchain-based applications.

![Meetup "Play with Blockchain" @SAKURA innobase Okinawa.png](../images/Meetup_Play_with_Blockchain_SAKURA_innobase_Okinawa_9d816744ec.png)

Based on the book "[Quick Learning Symbol JavaScript version](https://nextpublishing.jp/book/17872.html)", a working engineer will provide explanations as the course progresses.
This is an opportunity to learn about, develop and interact with blockchain, let's take a look and get involved.

### Meetup "Play with Blockchain" @SAKURA innobase Okinawa
* Date and time : Sunday, 24 May 2025, 13:00-18:00
* Venue : [SAKURA innobase Okinawa](https://www.sakura.ad.jp/innobase/)
* Address : [1F Haseko Naha Building, 1-2-13 Matsuyama, Naha City, Okinawa, Japan](https://maps.app.goo.gl/CJLhgyM98XWzhc7n7)
* Participation fees : Free of charge
