August 26, 2023 Open Developers Conference 2023 (ODC2023) will be held at a real venue for the first time in four years. 
ODC2023 is a place for developers and infrastructure engineers to communicate, providing a variety of the latest information based on the keywords "DevOps," "development," and "developers."
At the NEMTUS booth, they will introduce the NEM/Symbol blockchain technology, introduce community activities (information dissemination/study sessions/hackathons/other technical events), distribute goods, and exhibit and sell technical doujinshi. Mr. Yasunori Matsuoka will be on stage from 17:00-17:45 in Room B. You will take a lecture on "Introduction of design patterns in system development using blockchain". 
[ODC2023 Seminar Program: Introduction of design patterns in system development using blockchain](https://event.ospn.jp/odc2023/session/1090945) 
In addition, “Symbol Blockchain Development Experience Hands-on” will also be held at the same venue on the same day. If you have a computer that can run Git and Node.js and an editor such as VSCode, you will have a blockchain experience with executable content.
