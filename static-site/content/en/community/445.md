[NEMTUS](https://nemtus.com)  will co-host a beginner-friendly blockchain study session with [Ryukoku University Programming Club Horizon](https://x.com/ryukokuhorizon) and [Momoyama Gakuin University Tech Club](https://x.com/momoyama_tech).

![Blockchain Study Session.png](../images/Blockchain_Study_Session_6c60808c91.png)

* First half: A simple explanation of "What are Web3 and blockchain?"
* Second half: Hands-on experience with "[Poiny](https://symbol-community.com/community/337)"  - a blockchain-based app and an explanation of its technical logic!

This event is easy to understand. It is suitable for technical beginners and students. It offers learning through use cases and hands-on experience.

### Event schedule
* Date: Saturday, 28 June 2025
* Time: 1:00 PM - 4:25 PM(followed by an after-party until 5:30 PM for those who wish to stay)
* Venue: Ryukoku University, Fukakusa Campus, Room J303, 3rd Floor
* Address: [67 Tsukamoto-cho, Fukakusa, Fushimi-ku, Kyoto City ](https://maps.app.goo.gl/nNpgq2RbZu3d6ozb9)
* Participation fee: Free 
* What to bring: Laptop and charger
