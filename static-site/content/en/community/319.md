### Overview
NEMTUS has announced its participation in IVS2024 KYOTO as a community partner, and also informed that it will offer free tickets or voucher codes to interested community members who participate in NEMTUS' activities. This is a great opportunity to get in touch with the evolution and application of blockchain technology, learn about the latest technological trends and interact with industry leaders. 

Please contact NEMTUS [support@nemtus.com] if you are interested in free tickets or voucher codes. 

### About IVS Crypto 2024
IVS Crypto 2024 is Japan's largest cryptocurrency conference and an international event dedicated to the blockchain and cryptocurrency industry.IVS Crypto 2024 will be held as part of IVS2024 KYOTO, one of the largest startup conferences in Japan, and will take place from 4th to 6th July 2024 at KYOTO PULSE PLAZA. This year's theme is "Cross the Boundaries", offering a moment where tradition and innovation merge, where Japanese creativity meets new perspectives from around the world. Web3 entrepreneurs, investors and developers from around the world are invited to discuss the latest technology trends and business opportunities. For more information, please visit [IVS2024 KYOTO](https://www.ivs.events).

### About NEMTUS
[NEMTUS](https://nemtus.com) is a non-profit organisation dedicated to the research, development and promotion of blockchain technology. NEMTUS works with national and international companies and organisations to promote the social implementation of blockchain through promoting the technology, the provision of solutions for businesses and educational activities. It is expected to make a significant contribution to the creation of a new economic space.

### Event Summary
- Date: Thursday 4 - Saturday 6 July 2024
- Venue： KYOTO PULSE PLAZA
- Theme： "Cross the Boundaries" － Where we bring Japan's rich heritage and latest innovations to a global platform.
