Event title: [NexTech Week Tokyo 2025 6th Blockchain Expo Spring Edition](https://www.nextech-week.jp/hub/ja-jp/visit/bc.html)
Date: 15-17 Apr 2025
Venue: Tokyo Big sight
Admission: Free (pre-registration required)

![Blockchain Expo 2025 Spring.png](../images/Blockchain_Expo_2025_Spring_103a077fe1.png)

A total of 12 companies and organisations, including NEMTUS, a non-profit organisation that aims to promote and develop NEM and Symbol technologies in Japan, and companies/organisations that are developing businesses using Symbol, will exhibit together at the event "NexTech Week Tokyo 2025 6th Blockchain Expo Spring Edition", the largest event in Japan for the latest blockchain technologies and services. 

The exhibition is a collection of diverse case studies from companies that are actually using the symbol blockchain, and by showcasing their products and services in one place, the exhibition will comprehensively promote its technological potential and value for social implementation. It also aims to solve problems, apply it to existing businesses and create new businesses and collaborations.

The AI, Quantum Computing and Human Resourcing in Digital Development Support EXPO will also be held at the same venue. And the showcase is expected to be an excellent opportunity for Symbol to reach out the many business people interested in the latest digital technologies.
