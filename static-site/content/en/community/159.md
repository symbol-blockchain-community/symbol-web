NEMTUS, a non-profit organisation that aimed to the promotion and development of NEM and Symbol blockchain technologies, has become a cooperation partner of the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2023 Yokohama, which will take place on Sunday 7 October 2023.

![NASA INTERNATIONAL SPACE APPS CHALLENGE](../images/5_D9k_g1k_jpg_small_d7e45cbd01.jpeg)

The NASA Space Apps Challenge is a hackathon event organised by NASA to address social issues using satellite observations and open data. A cooperation partner, NEMTUS, is planning the NEMTUS Prize for the hackathon.
