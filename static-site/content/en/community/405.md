'CHRONOTH' is a new blogging platform built on the Symbol, combining ease of use with full data persistence via the blockchain.
* [CHRONOTH](https://chronoth.nftdrive.net/CHRONOTH/67BBD9D2C7F98C39120D7BC4)

![CHRONOTH_2.jpeg](../images/CHRONOTH_2_e5fa50379e.jpeg)

### **Features**

* Data persistence:
    * Blockchain technology ensures that all data and information, such as article, are stored in an unalterable and permanent manner.
    * The Symbol blockchain offers a high level of security and a large data storage capacity.
    * It focuses on data persistence and aims to solve the problems faced by traditional blogging platforms.
* User Friendly:
    * No programming or web3 skills are required to browse the simple interface.
    * The author of the article is required to use an 'NFTDriveEX' wallet due to its blockchain-based nature.
* Rewards for contributions:
    * CHRONOTH tokens are awarded based on the amount of data that is posted and the number of impressions.
    
### **Technology**
* Symbol blockchain:
    * It has high security, which eliminates the risk of exploiting vulnerabilities in the smart contract programme, and sufficient large data processing capacity for storage on the blockchain.
* NFTDrive:
    * NFTDrive's patent-pending technology makes data tampering impossible by discarding the private key after data is stored.

### **Outlook for the CHRONOTH**
* The practicality of the system as a digital archive has been presented at conferences[ at the Japan Society for Digital Archive](https://symbol-community.com/community/365) and has proved to be a very successful one.
* It presents the possibility of complete data storage through blockchain technology, and aims to be a platform for connecting human knowledge to the future.
