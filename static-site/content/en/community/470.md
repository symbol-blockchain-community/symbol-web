## Event overview
On Saturday 29 November, [NEMTUS](https://nemtus.com)  is hosting a hands-on workshop on blockchain application development in Sendai, Miyagi Prefecture.
![NEMTUS_Sendai_handson.jpeg](../images/NEMTUS_Sendai_handson_f2905c1c87.jpeg)
### Details
Use the Symbol blockchain to develop an application that can send messages from the LINE app to the blockchain.
The day will include both hands-on practice and case studies on regional revitalisation and Web3.

* Date and time: Saturday, 29 November 2025, 2pm–6pm (registration from 1.45pm)
* Venue: [Millions Room 01, 2nd Floor, New Life Kakyoin, 1-1-30 Kakyoin, Aoba Ward, Sendai City, Miyagi Prefecture](https://maps.app.goo.gl/CfBnbXh2j3PqxLDN8).
* Participation fee: Free
What to bring: A laptop computer (both Mac and Windows are acceptable) and a charging cable.

### Recommended for those who...
* Wish to start using or try out blockchain technology
* Web engineers and app developers interested in new technologies
* Those interested in the Symbol blockchain or Web3
* People interested in regional revitalisation combined with technology
