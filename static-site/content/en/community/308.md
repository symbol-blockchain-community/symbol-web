Event title: [NexTech Week Tokyo 2024 5th Blockchain Expo Spring Edition](https://www.nextech-week.jp/hub/ja-jp/visit/bc.html)
Date: 22-24 May 2024
Venue: Tokyo Big Sight West Exhibition Hall
Admission: Free (pre-registration required)

A total of 11 companies and organisations, including NEMTUS, a non-profit organisation that aims to promote and develop NEM and Symbol technologies in Japan, and companies/organisations that are developing businesses using Symbol, will jointly exhibit at the event "NexTech Week Tokyo 2024 5th Blockchain Expo Spring Edition", the largest event in Japan for the latest blockchain technologies and services. 

They will showcase the products and services of companies/organisations using the Symbol blockchain, comprehensively highlight the technical potential of the Symbol blockchain and the value of its social implementation, and explore the adoption and collaboration.

The AI, Quantum Computing and Human Resourcing in Digital Development Support EXPO will also be held at the same venue. And the showcase is expected to be an excellent opportunity for Symbol to reach out the many business people interested in the latest digital technologies.
