Opening Line Inc. is pleased to announce that it has been selected to participate in Microsoft Corporation's ISV Success Programme, which is designed to support the development of cloud services.

The programme supports companies that develop and deliver by using Microsoft solutions by providing development tools, technical support, and marketing assistance to expand distribution channels.
Opening Line plans to use the programme to expand distribution channels for its products JUGGLE, Green Life Point and Machi Meguri, as well as accelerate the development of new blockchain products. The company will use the tools, consulting and marketing support provided by the Microsoft ISV Success Program to strengthen its product development and sales strategy.

**About the 'JUGGLE'** 
The 'JUGGLE' is a secure file-sharing service that automatically generates highly secure passwords for file sharing by integrating blockchain and encryption technologies to provide highly confidential file sharing and a convenient user experience. Opening Line Inc. aims to further improve the quality of its services by incorporating Azure OpenAI Service, and will continue to grow its business to accelerate not only a secure file-sharing service, but also information sharing in the business.

**About the 'Green Life Point'** 
The 'Green Life Point' is part of the 'Green Life Point for Food and Life' project which is promoted by Japan's Ministry of the Environment, which encourages people to protect the environment by earning points for eco-friendly activities. The public blockchain is used for the points usage part. Users can earn points through environmental activities and use them in various ways. Microsoft Corporation's ISV Success Programme will be used to further develop this business.
In February 2023, this initiative was listed as a Microsoft Startup Case Study. Specific examples can be found in the 'Startups Case Study Compendium [February 2023 edition].' By partnering with the ISV Success Programme, OpeningLine Inc. hopes to encourage even more people to use Green Life Points so that each individual's eco-friendly activities contribute to the sustainability of the entire planet. The company hopes to create a world in which each individual's eco-friendly activities contribute to the sustainability of the entire planet.

**About the 'Machi Meguri'** 
This is an application based on FiFiC, a health promotion application based on blockchain technology that aims to collect stamps at spots to promote health and revitalise communities through touring. The application is currently in the development and test marketing phase, but will be further accelerated through the ISV Success Programme.
