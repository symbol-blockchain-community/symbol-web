Quick Learning Symbol, an introduction to the Symbol blockchain, is a guide to learning how to use the Symbol blockchain to enable contracts, token issuance, multi-level multi-sig functionality, encrypted messaging and more using the blockchain, and is currently available in English, Chinese (both traditional and simplified) and Italian, as well as Japanese.
The only environment required for development is a browser, allowing even novice programmers to try their hand at developing blockchain applications.

[Quick Learning Symbol Japanese Version](https://learn.ja.symbol-community.com/)
[Quick Learning Symbol English Version](https://learn.en.symbol-community.com/)
[Quick Learning Symbol Chinese traditional Version](https://learn.zh-tw.symbol-community.com)
[Quick Learning Symbol Chinese Simplified Version](https://learn.zh-cn.symbol-community.com)
[Quick Learning Symbol Italian Version](https://learn.it.symbol-community.com)

## Background to multilingualization

Originally, the Quick Learning Symbol was written by [XEMBook](https://twitter.com/xembook) in Japanese and used as learning content for study groups and to introduce blockchain to engineers.

Then, following feedback from international members, it was multilingualised in collaboration with native speakers of each language in the Symbol community.

## Future outlook

The Symbol community aims to bring the benefits of blockchain to as many people as possible, as stated in 'Empowering People with Blockchain'. In the future, we will support translation into more languages by working with native speakers of the target language and responding in the language in which contributors are available.

The translation work will be done by community members. If you would like to contribute to the translation, please [contact](https://twitter.com/symnem_com_info) us.
