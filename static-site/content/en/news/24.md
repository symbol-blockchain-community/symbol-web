## Welcome to the evolution Join the HACK+

The 'NEMTUS Hackathon HACK+ 2023' is an event where participants bring their manifold and unique ideas and technologies to compete and thereby make the NEM/Symbol community and its economic sphere will become more active and the aim of the hackathon event is to accelerate the spread of the technology.  

## Themes: Connection ～Connecting the world～

Please use a theme in NIS1/Symbol/mijin as the subject of your submissions and submit utilities or interacting chain systems. 

We are looking for works that connect to the real world and existing services.
Please show your creativity in the rules of participation.  

## Prizes

* Grand Prize 35.000 XYM
* Award for Excellence 20.000 XYM  (Will be increased as the number of entries increases)
* Special Prize 15.000 XYM (Will be increased as the number of entries increases)
