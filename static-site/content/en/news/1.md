## Launch

We are pleased to announce the launch of **Symbol Community Web**. The Symbol Community Web will continue to be active in order to make it easier and safer for newcomers to access information, to participate, to connect the already active with the new and to innovate.

Everyone is welcome to participate in the maintenance and development of Symbol Community Web. If you are interested in working to make this technology more widely available, please contact us at [Discord](https://discord.gg/TT2tvxFfN4). 

We are open to people with different ideas, actively discussing and working for the betterment of OSS.
