As part of its efforts to promote blockchain in the education sector, Opening Line Inc. organised a blockchain course for parents in partnership with ITeens Lab, which runs a comprehensive online IT school for primary and secondary school students nationwide, with a focus on programming.

Opening Line Inc. plans to continue to organise blockchain seminars in collaboration with companies and educational institutions, with the aim of achieving a "safe, secure and cooperative" society through the use of blockchain technology.
