NEMTUS (Non-Profit Organisation for the Promotion of NEM Technology) will appear in a webinar on 28 June 2023 by Caica Exchange, managing the crypto asset exchange Zaif. On 21 June 2023, CAICA DIGITAL Inc. announced an airdrop of NEM to CAICA Coin (CICC) holders. 
NEMTUS will present the attractiveness of the open source blockchain platform 'NEM', information from a technical perspective and future prospects. 

[Event overview] 
- Speaker ：Shin Suzuki(CAICA DIGITAL Inc. CEO/CAICA exchange Inc. CEO), Hiroyuki Goto(NEMTUS President)Yasunori Matuoka(NEMTUS VIce President) 
- Date ： (Wed)28th June 2023 19:00 to 20:00 
- Content ：Airdrop of NEM(XEM) to CAICA Coin (CICC) holders. The 4th air drop information on（NEM/XEM）
